About BIOSH

BIOSH is more than just another social platform. We’re built on a foundation of user empowerment and privacy.

With BIOSH, you log in via SSH—just like accessing a secure server. This means there are no emails or passwords to worry about. Instead, you use a public/private key pair for authentication, ensuring that only you can access your account.

We’re also deeply committed to protecting your privacy beyond just authentication. BIOSH is proudly hosted with Hetzner, a privacy-focused provider that isn’t part of the big tech conglomerates. By choosing Hetzner, we ensure that our servers remain secure, our data practices transparent, and your personal information completely under your control.

Our approach combines state-of-the-art encryption, secure key management, and trusted hosting to provide a social platform where you can connect without compromising your privacy.
BIOSH is designed for those who value both technical excellence and personal security.
