BIOSH Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2/21/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BIOSH! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

BIOSH operates with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information strictly for platform functionality:

  • Account Information: Your username and public SSH key.
  • IP Addresses: Collected for security purposes, including preventing abuse and managing platform integrity.
  • Usage Data: Basic logs for security and troubleshooting (no personal tracking).

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data collected to:

  • Provide access to the BIOSH platform.
  • Ensure platform security and functionality.
  • Monitor and prevent abuse, including potential bans for violating community guidelines.
  • Troubleshoot issues and improve user experience.

4. Data Protection and Security

We prioritize user privacy through:

  • End-to-end encryption of communications.
  • Secure storage of SSH keys and account data.
  • Regular security audits to prevent vulnerabilities.

5. User Control and Data Access

As a BIOSH user, you control your data:

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • No personal information is stored beyond what is required for account access.

6. Third-Party Services

BIOSH does not rely on third-party services for user data storage or processing. Your activity remains within the BIOSH environment.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of BIOSH after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at bioshofficial@proton.me.
